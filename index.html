Future Work

Plan for Future

The Concept

The Concept is a collective name for some yet interesting future projects of the Creators Community. This will directly reflects the curiosity, enthusiasm and passion to collaborate with each other to learn, implement and explore the creativity, to embark the possibilities of dumb yet interesting ideas.

Read More

01. DevCorps Book

DevCorps Book is a collection of stories, ideas, innovations, articles, events organized by sister communities of Herald Devcorps Creators Community and other students inside Herald College Kathmandu. Devcorps Book will start with the origin story of Herald Devcorps, and its child communities and will be updated every year.

Read More

02. Creators Forum

Creators Forum is a HCK based stack overflow which will be operated by the students, for the students of Herald College Kathmandu. Creators Forum will be a platform to question and answer by the real students of the college. It features questions and answers on a wide range of topics in computer programming.

Read More

03. Behind The Abstraction

Behind The Abstraction is a conceptual process where the background of in general rules and concepts are derived from the usage and classification of specific examples. Here, the successful identities and products will be a postmortem and their backgrounds from ground to top story will be explored.

Read More

04. Crash Course: New Slides

Creators Crash Course is a three day camp which will be dedicated to early birds to prepare them for the 3 years course of Herald College Kathmandu by summarizing the course which will be delivered by an alumni.

Read More

05. Virtual Herald

Virtual Herald is a 3D model mapping of Herald College Kathmandu which will sync the real life experience of our college. This will transform real-life spaces into immersive digital twin models.

Read More

06. Creators Task Force

Creators Task Force is a group of people who are willing to learn but don't want to be involved in the community. They will be learning as well as volunteering for Creators Community and will be the inactive members but Creators Community will be approaching this group of people which we will need to contribute to the community.

Read More

07. Herald Brain

Herald Brain is a research initiative to work on WOT, IOT, semantic web, expert systems, swarm intelligence, complex systems, collaborative development with their practical implementation in Nepal.

Read More

08. Herald Talent Fest

Herald Talent Fest is a tournament-based fest/event in which all freestyle, raw and trained talents can showcase their skills of any type in order to proceed further in the tournament.

Read More

09. Herald Incubation Center

Herald Incubation Center is a small organization under Herald DevCorps: Creators Community, created to provide space for the individuals who are willing to discover and experiment with numerous projects.
